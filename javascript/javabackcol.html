PARIS

What makes Paris the "City of Love" is not just its beautiful sights, but the way the city makes people feel.


Paris invites people to slow down, to savour each moment, and to fall in love not only with each other but with the city itself.



“The City of Light” (“la Ville Lumière”), earned during the Enlightenment, remains appropriate,



for Paris has retained its importance as a centre for education and intellectual pursuits.
